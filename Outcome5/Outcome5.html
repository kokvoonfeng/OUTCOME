2. Describe the advantage of ‘transparency’ when applied to PNG over GIF.

Answer:

PNG has three main advantages over GIF, first alpha channels variable transparency , PNG also compression better than GIF in almost every case and the GIF format can also be interlaced, but the quality is not nearly as good, or fast as a PNG. Graphics file formats There are two different ways to support transparent image areas such as simple binary transparency and alpha transparency. The GIF format supports simple binary transparency, and a color in the index palette is marked as a transparent color. The PNG format supports alpha transparency, and transparency information is stored on a separate channel.
